Our Project Description

The team is working on the JKUAT Department of Computing Website Enhancement Initiative, aiming to modernize and improve the department's online presence by redesigning and upgrading its official website. This project focuses on enhancing user experience, accessibility, and content organization to better serve students, faculty, staff, prospective students, and external stakeholders.

Through a comprehensive overhaul, we will create a user-friendly platform featuring interactive elements, multimedia content, and responsive design. Our goal is to showcase the department's academic programs, research activities, faculty profiles, news and events, and achievements effectively, ensuring a seamless and engaging digital experience for all visitors.

Solution

The team will add lecturer profiles to help in identification and easier recognition by students and visitors of the website. These profiles contain high quality photographs. The lecturers research work is also included besides the projects they have done or been involved in. The website will also feature a section of news and events that are up-to-date and of high quality
